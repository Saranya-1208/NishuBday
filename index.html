Dearest Nishchithaaaaa,

Happy Birthday, my child. I am so incredibly proud of the person you are becoming — the grace with which you carry yourself, and the love you pour into everything you choose to do. You were incredible, and I was certain you would fit right into the tiny world of NSRCEL we all cherished so much. And you didn't just fit in, you made it your own, with such ease and so much love. There was so much we laughed about, learned about, and absolutely bitched about, and I wouldn't trade a single moment of it.

My last year at NSRCEL was magic because you were in it. I leaned on you as much as you leaned on me, and I would not have it any other way. All the gossip, the Amul runs, the Wordle battles, the spelling errors, crying before presentations, staying late to "work" — everything was ten times more fun with you cackling next to us.

I am so grateful to have found a friend in you, Nishu. I am so proud of how much you hold, you do SO MUCH, and you do it all with such heart. I cannot wait to roam around Bangalore in your gaadi, go on ice cream runs, and laugh at all the silly, wonderful things we find around us.

And on the days you find yourself second-guessing your talents, just know that I still have your CF velocity doodle cards (I forget what they're actually called, soz) proudly sitting on my desk, and I tell everyone that the most talented girl made these.
